Game Description: 	
	Die Bremer Stadtmusikanten: The Road to Bremen is a top-down medieval adventure inspired by the classic fairy tale. You play as four old animals — the donkey, dog, cat, and rooster — who have been cast aside by their masters and set out together on the road to Bremen. The world is built around a dark medieval atmosphere: small villages, forest roads, river crossings, robber camps, and the streets of Bremen itself. Each animal has a different ability, and the player must switch between them to solve obstacles, avoid enemies, and keep the group moving forward. The game is about teamwork, survival, and the idea that even those who are forgotten can still find a purpose. At its heart, the game tells a simple story: four weak creatures become stronger together than they ever were alone. Their journey ends in Bremen, where they finally reach the city and earn their place as the Bremen Town Musicians. 

Game Controls: 
	W - Move up 
	D - Move right 
	S - Move down 
	A - Move left 
	F - Play music/Collect animals 
	Q - Stack all animals on each other 
	E - Use lead animal's ability 
	1 - Choose donkey 
	2 - Choose dog 
	3 - Choose cat 
	4 - Choose rooster
